# LinkedIn Hook Writer Skill

Generate pattern-breaking hooks for LinkedIn posts.

## Trigger

- Manual: `/linkedin-hook-writer` or "write hooks for this"
- Automatic: Step 4 of the Content Flywheel pipeline

## The Hook's Job

The first 2 lines readers see before clicking "... more" on LinkedIn.

**Constraints:**
- 2 short sentences
- ~55 characters max each
- Must break scrolling pattern
- Must create an open loop

**Anti-patterns:**
- Personal achievements ("I just hit 10K followers")
- Emoji openers (🚀 💡 ✨)
- Hashtags
- Generic statements ("Here's what I learned")

## 5 Hook Techniques

### 1. Contradiction
Say something that sounds wrong.

### 2. Specific Number + Unexpected Context
Combine a number with something surprising.

### 3. Direct Accusation
Call the reader out.

### 4. Stolen Thought
Say what the reader secretly thinks but won't say.

### 5. Absurd Reframe
Take something mundane and make it dramatic.

## Workflow

### Step 1: Understand the Content

From the audited content, identify:
- The core insight
- The pain point
- The unexpected truth
- The specific number or detail

### Step 2: Generate 10 Hooks

2 hooks per technique, organized as:

```
## HOOK OPTIONS

### Technique 1: Contradiction
1A. [line 1]
    [line 2]
1B. [line 1]
    [line 2]

[... continue for all 5 techniques]
```

### Step 3: Character Count

Verify each line is ~55 chars or less.

### Step 4: Recommend Top 3

Based on:
- Pattern-breaking strength
- Relevance to content
- Authenticity

## Quality Standards

1. **Pattern-breaking** — Must stop the scroll
2. **Under 55 chars** per line
3. **About reader** — Not about the author
4. **No AI patterns** — No "Let's dive in" energy
5. **Authentic** — Sounds like a real person
