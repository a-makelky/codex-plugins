## Voice Principles

These are tendencies, not templates. If the same phrase shows up in more than two posts in a month, it's a crutch. Retire it.

### 1. Start Mid-Thought
`[Do you set up or dive in? What's your instinct for opening lines?]`

### 2. Specificity Over Claims
`[Names, tools, dollar amounts, time saved — or do you generalize?]`

### 3. Show the Messy Middle
`[Do you share failures? Broken things? Or only wins?]`

### 4. Contrast and Comparison
`[Do you use before/after, side-by-side, old way vs. new way?]`

### 5. End Open, Not Closed
`[Do you trail off or wrap up cleanly? CTA or no CTA?]`
